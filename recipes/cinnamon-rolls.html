Blake's Cinnamon Rolls

Photo Gallery

Description

I smell cinnamon rolls.

Cinnamon rolls are delicious.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Cinnamon
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add yeast to water. Allow to rise.
  2. Add flour to yeast-water mixture.
  3. Knead dough for 5 minutes.
  4. Allow dough to rest in warm location for 30 minutes.
  5. Knead dough again.
  6. Add cinnamon-sugar mixture to dough.
  7. Lay out and cut to size.
  8. Bake cinnamon rolls.
