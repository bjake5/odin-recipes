Blake's Bagel Sandwich

Photo Gallery

Description

I love bagel sandwiches with egg and avocado.

Bagel sandwiches are delicious.

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs. Add salt, pepper, and garlic. Microwave for 1 minute.
  2. Dice the avocado.
  3. Place egg and avocado on bagel.
